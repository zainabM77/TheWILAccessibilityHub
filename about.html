About the Toolkit

This toolkit addresses gaps in accessibility and communication within clinical placements.

Who is this for?

Students, faculty, Clinical Course Directors, and preceptors.

Key Challenges

  • Unclear accommodation processes
  • Poor communication
  • Limited awareness
  • Barriers such as fatigue and stigma

Our Approach

We use a before-during-after framework for continuous support.
