Preparing for Placement

Taking proactive steps reduces stress and improves your experience.

Your Rights

  • Right to Equality
  • Duty to Accommodate
  • Privacy and Confidentiality
  • Freedom from Reprisal

Checklist

  • Register with Accessibility Services
  • Request Letter of Accommodation
  • Identify site contact
  • Submit requests early

Questions to Ask

  • Who is my main contact?
  • How are accommodations shared?
  • What if issues arise?
